About Rainnie

Rainie began her career in 2000, as a member of Taiwanese quartet girl group 4 in Love, where she was given the stage name of Rainie, matching the weather theme of each member.After the group disbanded in 2002, Yang continued to pursue a career in the entertainment industry as a TV host, hosting shows.She played supporting roles in serverl Taiwanese dramas, including the Meteor Garden.

2005

Rainie landed the lead role, as Qi Yue, in the CTV drama Devil Beside You opposite Mike He in 2005.

2007

Rainie returned to her previous cute image by becoming the lead actress in the drama Why Why Love, co-starring with Mike He and Kingone Wang.She nominated 43rd Golden Bell Awards for Best Leading Actress because of this drama.

2009

Rainie acted as the main chacarter inHi My Sweetheart and ToGetHer.She gained the 45th Golden Bell Awards for Best Leading Actress.
